FIRST Tech Challenge students learn to think like engineers. Teams design, build, and code robots to compete in an alliance format against other teams. Robots are built from a reusable platform, powered by Android technology, and can be coded using a variety of levels of Java-based programming.

Our Journey

We are The Eagles 21028, the robotics team that proudly represents "Roman-Vodă" National College in the FIRST® Tech Challenge Romania competition, since 2018. In the CenterStage season we won the Iași Regional by being the Winning Alliance Captain. Our team also earned the Control Award - 1st Place for our robot's auto period performance.
